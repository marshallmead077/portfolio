About Me

Hello, my name is Marshall Mead, and I am a graduate theater major and a seasoned musician. I will graduate this week on December 18th and I have been interested in working in the field of voice editing for a while now. I have worked with recording software and running live sound for about 3 years. I also have been working on being a sound designer through my college time. I have also worked with ADR at an internship with Lucky Dogs Audio Post.I would like to continue working with voice work and begin pursuing this goal and taking on projects related to animation. Not only that, but I think this would be a great start for me because this type of work is what I have always wanted to do.
